Make a reservation

You can request a reservation by filling in the form below. We will get back to you by email as soon as possible to confirm your booking.
Please note that your reservation is only confirmed once you receive our confirmation email.

For groups larger than four, a phone number is required. You can also reserve by phone on the days we are open, starting in the afternoon. Please keep in mind that we are closed on Mondays and Tuesdays.
Requests made on that day will be answered on our next open day, and same-day reservations can only be accepted until 15:00.
